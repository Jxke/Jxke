New Media Artist · Creative Technologist · Design Engineer · Educator
--

<i>Jake Tan (1994) </i> investigates the sublime from scientific phenomena at the intersection of living systems and digital frameworks.
<br>
Data is treated as material, translated into digi-physical works spanning hardware, spatial media, and interactive software.
<br>
My public repositories consist of technologies created for my artworks, projects I'm a part of and workshops I've conducted.

Grad Student — Harvard University, Master in Design Engineering
<br>
Co-Founder — [SERIAL CO_ ](https://github.com/serialco)
<br>
Core Member — [CREATIVE TECH SINGAPORE](https://github.com/ct-sg)
